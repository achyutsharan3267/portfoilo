Project 1 Fan Control Football

Fan Controlled Football is a dormant professional 7-on-7 indoor football league that played two seasons in 2021 and 2022. The league operated on the premise of fans voting on play calls and other in-game events. All games were played at the Pullman Yards in Atlanta, Georgia and broadcast on streaming television.

Project 4 CloudSnooker

Pan American Billiards and Snooker Association along with CloudSnooker are promoting a automated club running app where by the club can be run using a custom tablet app, which allows sign in and automatic charges for the table time as well as purchases made in the club to its members. Members will have to sign on to Cloudsnooker and their accounts for payment will be securely done using stripe.
